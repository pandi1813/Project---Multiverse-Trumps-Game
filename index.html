Heroes & Villains Epic Faceoff

The most epic battle between heroes and villains from every universe ever!!!

How to play?

  • - There are 10 rounds in the game.
  • - At the start of each round, the player and computer will each receive a random hero/villain card.
  • - The player shall choose a power from their character to battle against the computer's character.
  • - The character with the higher power value shall win the round!
  • - The winner will gain a point, while no point is awarded for a tie or loss.
  • - The side with the most points after 10 rounds wins the game!
